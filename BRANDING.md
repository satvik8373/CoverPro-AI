# ApplyGenius - Professional Branding Guide

## App Name
**ApplyGenius** - A professional, meaningful name that conveys intelligence and expertise in job applications.

## Tagline
"Craft compelling job application emails with AI precision. Upload your resume and job details for instant, personalized outreach."

## Key Features Messaging

### Professional Language
- **Document Upload** (instead of "Upload Files")
- **Professional Profile** (instead of "Personal Information") 
- **Job Posting** (instead of "Job Description Image")
- **Professional Resume** (instead of "Your Resume")
- **Application Email** (instead of "Perfect Email")

### Feature Highlights
- **Intelligent Email Crafting** - Emphasizes AI-powered analysis
- **Professional Templates** - Highlights quality and professionalism
- **Instant Delivery** - Emphasizes speed and efficiency

## Visual Identity
- **Primary Color**: Blue (#4F46E5) - Professional and trustworthy
- **Accent Color**: Teal (#059669) - Modern and sophisticated
- **Icon**: Combination of checkmark, document, and clock elements
- **Typography**: Inter (body), Space Grotesk (headlines)

## User Experience Improvements
- Clear 2-step process with visual progress
- Professional terminology throughout
- Enhanced file upload experience
- Better error messaging and guidance
- Mobile-responsive design

## Technical Branding
- Package name: `applygenius`
- LocalStorage keys: `applyGenius*`
- Component names: `ApplyGenius*`
- Professional metadata and descriptions

## Messaging Tone
- Professional yet approachable
- Confident and competent
- Focus on career success
- Emphasize quality and precision
- Avoid casual language

This rebranding positions ApplyGenius as a professional tool for serious job seekers who want to make the best impression with their applications.
